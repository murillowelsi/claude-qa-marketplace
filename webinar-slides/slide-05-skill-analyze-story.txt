================================================================================
  SLIDE 5: SKILL DEEP DIVE — /analyze-story

================================================================================

  THE PROBLEM IT SOLVES
  ---------------------
  Stories often reach QA with vague acceptance criteria, missing edge cases,
  and untestable requirements. QA engineers spend hours in refinement meetings
  asking the same questions. Defects get discovered in production instead
  of caught during story review.

  WHAT THE SKILL DOES
  -------------------
  1. Fetches the Jira ticket (including linked Confluence pages and epics)
  2. Evaluates across 5 quality dimensions:
     - INVEST criteria (Independent, Negotiable, Valuable, Estimable,
       Small, Testable)
     - Clarity and understandability
     - Acceptance Criteria quality
     - Testability and completeness
     - Consistency, feasibility, and dependencies
  3. Rates each issue: Critical / High / Medium / Low severity
  4. Rewrites weak or missing AC with [REVISED] and [NEW] labels
  5. Delivers a verdict: Ready / Needs Refinement / Not Ready
  6. Saves the report to qa-output/story-analysis/
  7. Optionally posts the analysis as a Jira comment or creates subtasks

  BEFORE THIS SKILL:
  ------------------
  QA engineer reads story, notices gaps manually during sprint,
  defects traced back to unclear requirements — too late.

  AFTER THIS SKILL:
  -----------------
  Run /analyze-story before the sprint starts.
  Gaps are caught in minutes, not weeks.
  Developers get clear, testable AC before writing a single line of code.

  COMMAND:
  --------
  /analyze-story PROJ-123
